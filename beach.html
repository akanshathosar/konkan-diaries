Beaches in Konkan

"Where sun meets sea — the magic of Konkan beaches."

Kihim Beach

“Kihim’s crimson canvas — a sunset worth pausing for.” A fiery orange sky lights up the rocks of Kihim Beach during sunset. This tranquil escape is known for its soft sands, tidal pools, and unforgettable twilight views.

Tarkarli Beach

“Tarkarli dreams — where the sea meets serenity.”Clear blue waters, powdery white sands, and swaying palm trees define Tarkarli — one of Maharashtra’s most pristine beaches. It’s a heaven for water sports and peaceful retreats.

Kashid Beach

“Golden hour at Kashid — where every stone tells a story.”As the sun sets over Kashid Beach, the tide recedes to reveal a bed of stones, creating a picture-perfect silhouette of nature.

Devghali Beach

“Where cliffs meet the sea — the raw beauty of Devghali Beach.” .A stunning blend of rocky cliffs and golden sands, the Devghali caves open right into the Arabian Sea.

Murud Beach

“Timeless in tide — the mighty Murud-Janjira at dusk.”Rising from the sea like a guardian of time, the Murud-Janjira Fort stands majestic against the backdrop of a glowing sunset.

Harihareshwar Beach

“Twin treasures of the Konkan — Diveagar’s calm and Harihareshwar’s charm.”A breathtaking coastal stretch known for sacred temples and serene beaches, Harihareshwar and Diveagar are perfect for a spiritual and scenic getaway.
