Who We Are

Konkan in My Heart, Adventure on My Mind

Welcome to Konkan Diaries, a personal travel blog where stories from the coastal soul of India come alive.

  • I'm Akansha Thosar, a travel enthusiast, storyteller, and explorer of offbeat paths—especially those that wind through the serene beauty of the Konkan region in Maharashtra.
  • This blog is born from my love for discovering hidden beaches, coastal food, local traditions, and heartwarming village life. Whether you're planning your first trip or just wandering through wanderlust, here you'll find practical tips, personal experiences, and curated guides to help you connect deeply with the places you visit.
  • Every post is written from firsthand experiences—with muddy shoes, salt in the air, and stories from locals who make the journey unforgettable.
