Temples in Konkan

"Konkan’s temples — where devotion meets tradition."

Kunkeshwar Temple

Famous for its scenic beauty, clean waters, and proximity to Mumbai, Alibaug Beach is perfect for a quick coastal getaway.

Ganpatipule Temple

Known for its crystal-clear water and water sports, Tarkarli is a paradise for divers and beach lovers alike.

Marleshwar Temple

Surrounded by Casuarina trees, Kashid is famous for its white sand and tranquil environment—ideal for peace seekers.

Vyadeshwar Temple

Popular for scuba diving and Sindhudurg Fort, Malvan Beach offers a perfect blend of history and marine life.

Parshuram Temple

“Gateway to divinity – Shri Parshuram Mandir, where legend and legacy meet.” An intricately carved entrance to the historic Shri Parshuram Temple, this structure reflects traditional Konkani architecture and deep spiritual heritage. The temple is dedicated to Lord Parshuram, the sixth avatar of Vishnu, and is a serene pilgrimage spot nestled in the lush Western Ghats

Karneshwar Temple

The Karneshwar Temple is an ancient Shiva temple known for its Hemadpanthi-style stone architecture. Surrounded by greenery, this lesser-known gem offers a peaceful retreat into heritage and devotion amidst nature’s lap.
